About me

I was born and raised in Toronto, Ontario. I knew that I wanted to go into engineering since I was a little kid and would do woodworking with my grandfather. The University of Waterloo's Mechatronics Engineering program was my top pick for undergrad because I have a strong inclination to do mechanical work, but I am adept at electrical work. Before starting university, I founded a woodworking company with my peer. I am a self-starter, and if I am interested in the work I am doing, I will not stop until I am satisfied with the results.

Currently, this is true regarding my design team. I started the Baja SAE design team at the University of Waterloo and am now the captain. This team had previously been run at the university but was disbanded before I started at the school. In just two weeks, I was able to get my team affiliated with Waterloo and accumulate nearly 100 members. When I'm not studying, I am doing work for Baja or teaching new members.
